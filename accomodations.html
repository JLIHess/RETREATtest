HOTEL AND TRANSPORTATION

Hotel

Atlanta Evergreen Marriott Conference Resort

4021 Lakeview Drive Stone Mountain, Georgia 30083 USA

Surround yourself with the beauty of Stone Mountain, GA at Atlanta Evergreen Marriott Conference Resort. Set within Stone Mountain Park – Georgia’s most visited attraction – The hotel offers newly renovated guestrooms with private balconies offering nature, pool, or lake views. Take a dip in the heated indoor or outdoor pool, work out in the 24/7 gym, or practice your swing on the 36-hole golf course. The lakefront conference center boasts 64,000 sq. ft. of indoor and outdoor venue space, perfect for weddings, trade shows, or conferences. Explore the attractions within Stone Mountain Park, including seasonal entertainment for children. Get outside for hiking, biking, or kayaking as you explore 3,200 acres of parkland.
